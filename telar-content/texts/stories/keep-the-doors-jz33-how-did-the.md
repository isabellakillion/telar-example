---
title: "How did the lawsuit play out?"
---

The Nexus claimed the Regents violated the Bagley-Keene Act, which “prohibits a quorum or majority of a public agency from deciding to take a particular action outside an open meeting.” According to the Nexus, they also claimed “a violation of the Public Records Act” because Wilson had refused to release records of telephone conversations with other regents before the vote. Despite widespread criticism, the State Supreme Court ruled against the Nexus in 1999.
